STEPH VASS

DJ | Producer

Steph Vass has been involved and in love with the electronic music scene for over 8 years. His musical style is rooted in melodic progression and spans a variety of genres. This diversity makes each one of his sets a unique experience to behold.

Over the years, he has shared lineups with music heavy weights such as Oliver Koletzki, Hyhenah, and Ryan Murgatroyd, among others. Steph's passion for music is clear to anyone watching him play. When behind the decks, he's in his element, and his unbridled delight spreads across the crowd like wildfire.
